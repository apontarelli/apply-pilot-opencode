## ABOUT

I'm a Senior Product Manager who turns complex operational workflows into products customers trust and businesses can scale. I work best where the problem is ambiguous, the blast radius is high, and the team needs fast iteration with engineers and users.

At Wrapbook, I moved across onboarding, accounting, and shared UI/data foundations for a production payroll and accounting platform. I typically stepped in when the work was high-stakes, cross-functional, and needed fast learning loops.

Key outcomes I led:
- Helped open Wrapbook's move into film/TV accounting by shipping a 0-to-1 accounting product across a 30+ engineer effort in 6 months
- Replaced one-off report builds with a reusable reporting framework across ~10 reports, cutting new report delivery from ~2-3 months to ~2-3 weeks
- Made large exports reliable and self-serve, eliminating a major reporting support issue for finance teams
- Secured access for 50,000+ active users through MFA rollout and Auth0 migration without disrupting payroll continuity
- Built a 50-state workers' comp rules engine that aligned billing with quotes and insurance guidance, reducing overcharges and manual review
- Introduced AI into high-trust finance workflows through natural-language reporting with guardrails and evals

I care a lot about understanding what users are actually trying to accomplish and building software that fits how they work. In accounting, that meant data density, speed, and keyboard-first interaction rather than generic consumer UX conventions.

I'm currently bootstrapping Hard Sets, a workout app focused on fast logging and better training decisions, and I'm open to full-time PM roles in platform, data/reporting, and AI products. I do my best work on lean teams where PMs prototype early, stay close to users, and ship quickly with strong engineering partnership.
