# STAR STORY BANK

Purpose:
- reusable interview prep for product judgment, stakeholder management, technical trade-offs, AI fluency, and strategic thinking
- lead with corporate experience; use side projects as supporting proof only
- keep claims specific, honest, and defensible

Use this before interviews:
1. Pick 6-8 stories from the bank.
2. Fill in Situation, Task, Action, Result, and Reflection.
3. Add the likely interviewer angle: execution, strategy, conflict, technical judgment, AI, leadership, failure, or metrics.
4. Rehearse the 90-second version and the 4-minute version.

## Coverage Checklist

Before an interview loop, confirm the selected stories cover:
- one technical trade-off
- one stakeholder conflict or pushback
- one ambiguous strategy call
- one failure, wrong assumption, or pivot
- one cross-team execution story
- one AI fluency story
- one metrics or business-impact story
- one user discovery story
